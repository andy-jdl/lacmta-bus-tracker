LACMTA Bus Tracker

A real time bus arrival tracker for LA Metro stops. Text your stop number to receive live arrival predictions.

GitHub Repo

Privacy Policy

LACMTA Bus Tracker does not sell, rent, or share your phone number or any personal information with third parties. Your number is used solely to send bus arrival information in response to your requests. Reply STOP at any time to opt out.

How to Opt In

No sign up required. Text any LA stop number to recieve real time bus arrivals. Messages are only sent in response to your messages.

How to Opt Out

This service only sends messages in response to your texts. Therefore, you will never recieve unsolicited messages.
